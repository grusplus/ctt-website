Turn Trello boards into 
websites with zero coding

Get the info on your Trello boards out there quickly. Perfect for blogs, roadmaps, cookbooks, events and anything that needs a simple website, fast.




 

How It Works

You select which board and which cards you want to publish. You can create a website from the entire board, or filter it by columns or labels. For example, you can have a kanban-style "Published" column (perfect for blogs) or use columns as categories and use a "Published" label. Or combine multiple boards into one website.

1. Set up Trello

Get your content on a Trello board. Or maybe you already have one.

2. Set up Netlify

We use Netlify for hosting, so you get all the benefits of their super-fast hosting, and easy management. You'll need a (free) Netlify account.

3. Publish

Publish manually, or have your website update nightly or hourly with your latest changes.
