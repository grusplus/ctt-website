Past, Present, and Future

You'll find everything you need in so-incredibly-obvious-why-would-you-do-it-any-other-way Past, Present and Future sections. Curious how your future is looking? Go to the Future section. It's so simple I just wasted your time telling you.

All projections are just that -- projections. They're based on your past behaviour and some clever maths, but beyond that we make no claim that it'll be in any way accurate. Count Your Money is a tool, not an advisor.

It's your data

Import and export your data as a CSV, so you can go from your existing spreadsheet (if you have one) or move to more complex software later if/when you're ready.

Also, the app doesn't require banking passwords, and doesn't contain any 3rd-party tracking scripts that might try to sneak a peek. What's yours is yours.
