How It Works

1. Set up Trello

Get your content on a Trello board. Or maybe you already have one.

2. Set up Netlify

We use Netlify for hosting, so you get all the benefits of their super-fast hosting, and easy management. You'll need a (free) Netlify account.

3. Publish

Publish manually, or have your website update nightly or hourly with your latest changes.

What if I sign up and I hate it?

You can try it for free so no take-backs if you pay for it. You can cancel any time though. If you cancel, you'll get a pro-rated partial month refund. And your sites will stay up, they just won't update.

Then we expect you to bore at least three friends gushing about it over social video chats.

Netlify looks terrifying, do I need that?

Yes, a Netlify account is required. Don't worry, you don't have to know what all the options in there mean. We'll highlight the important parts in our guides.

Technical questions

Can I get howmanyorangescanfitinmymouth.com?

Probably but we don't sell domains. You can buy or set up a domain in your Netlify account.

Can I make my own theme?

No. You can apply custom CSS to our slightly-above-average markup output though.

Your name scares me. Do you just use a crazy amount of HTML <table> tags?

It's just a name. We only use table tags where table tags make sense.

Can I download the generated HTML output to use somewhere else?

We don't offer a download feature yet, but you can scrape your own site pretty easily if you knew how to ask that question.
