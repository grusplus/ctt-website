How It Works

For Your Clients

Your client downloads the app for free from the app store and, using a code that we send them, unlocks and links it to you. Then they use the app as they normally would. They write in their journal, log their moods and emotions, and answer any questions that you can configure together.

Management is very simple - you invite them through your dashboard and we send them everything they need, including the code and detailed instructions on how to get setup.

 




swap_horizontal_circle

For You

You can review entries, any time, from anywhere. You can easily review entries for the past week (or as far back as you need), see their long-term and short-term data trends, or keep an eye out for alarming entries. You can use it for quick and easy preparation before a session, or dig into the data to find something specific.

A quick search lets you switch between clients, and lots of advanced search options help you find just what you're looking for.

Easy-to-use app

Grapefruit is very simple to use for a wide variety of situations. Almost everything can be customized so you can help your clients tailor it to their needs, and re-configure it as those needs evolve.

The client app is available for iPhone, iPad, Android, Mac and Windows so there's an app wherever your client wants it.
